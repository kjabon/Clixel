What is Clixel?

We are a website that allows users the chance to win without spending money!

The concept is simple - there is a hidden pixel somewhere on the sponsor board. Clicking will load a message from the sponsor. After the message plays, it is revealed whether you won the money. If not, the pixel changes locations and you can click on the board again.

We want to allow users the opportunity to win money in their spare time. Bored on the bus? Log in and click some pixels! In a dull meeting? Pull out your phone and click some pixels!

That's it! It's really that simple. Have the chance to win money in your spare time. So start clicking!

Requirements to Participate

In order to win, you must create an account. To create an account, you must follow the laws in your area. If it is illegal for you to participate in our website, DO NOT create an account. Your account will become void if it is discovered laws have been violated.
